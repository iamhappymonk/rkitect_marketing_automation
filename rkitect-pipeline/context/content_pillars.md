# rkitect.ai — Content Pillars

> Used by the Filter Agent to select today's topic.

## Pillar Weights

| Pillar | Weight |
|---|---|
| Education & Insight | 35% |
| Social Proof & Transformation | 25% |
| Inspiration & Trend | 20% |
| Behind-the-Product | 10% |
| CTA & Conversion | 10% |

## Education & Insight (35%)
Teaches the audience something about their workflow, AI rendering, or spatial intelligence.
- "How post-render AI segmentation works"
- "The hidden cost of re-rendering"
- Connect insight to rkitect.ai's thesis always.

## Social Proof & Transformation (25%)
Before/after, revision time savings, workflow transformation.
- "4 days → 4 hours pitch prep"
- Use specific numbers, not vague "faster."

## Inspiration & Trend (20%)
Emerging design aesthetics architects save and share.
- Japandi, biophilic, ultra-minimal
- Tie back to "how would you visualize this?"

## Behind-the-Product (10%)
Build journey, founder perspective, engineering choices.
- Authentic, specific, founder-voiced.

## CTA & Conversion (10%)
Free trial, Design Unit explainers, tier comparisons.
- Lead with value even in CTA posts.

## 3-Day Deduplication Rule
Same pillar must not repeat within 3 days (except Education: 2-day minimum).
