## Company

rkitect.ai is an AI-powered architectural rendering and visualization platform built by HappyMonk AI in Bengaluru, India. Designed for real architects, interior designers, property developers, and architecture students.

## Brand Essence

Fast, controllable architectural visualization for real design workflows.

## Brand Promise

Help architects and design teams turn sketches, plans, photos, models, and prompts into client-ready visuals and revisions without slow, expensive rendering loops.

## Category

**Agentic Spatial Intelligence** — not "AI rendering tool", not "rendering software."

## Product Name

- Platform: **rkitect.ai** (lowercase r, always)
- Segmentation agent: **Sektura** (branded name for post-render AI segmentation)
- Currency: **Design Units** (never "credits")

## Core Differentiator: Post-Render Segmentation (Sektura)

The Segment Reveal is rkitect.ai's most powerful visual moment:
A flat architectural render that gradually "lights up" as Sektura labels each surface — walls, floors, windows pulse into labeled, editable segments. This is the visual that no competitor can match.

**6-Step Workflow:**
1. User uploads input (sketch, photo, floor plan, CAD, or prompt).
2. rkitect.ai generates photorealistic render in seconds.
3. AI immediately reads and analyzes the rendered output.
4. AI automatically identifies and labels every major object: walls, windows, doors, flooring, furniture, lighting, materials.
5. User clicks any labeled object and modifies it: change material, color, style, or swap item entirely.
6. Changes apply instantly without re-rendering the entire scene.

## Hard Brand Rules (Every Agent Must Enforce)

1. **NEVER** call rkitect.ai an "AI render tool" or "rendering software"
2. **NEVER** use "credits" — always "Design Units"
3. **NEVER** hedge with "might", "perhaps", "could potentially", "we believe", "we think", "can help you"
4. **NEVER** use: "magical", "revolutionary", "effortless", "guaranteed", "perfect", "disrupt"
5. **ALWAYS** reinforce the category: "Agentic Spatial Intelligence" or equivalent thesis language
6. **ALWAYS** name the product "rkitect.ai" (lowercase r)
7. **ALWAYS** name the segmentation feature "Sektura"
8. **NEVER** position AI as replacing architects — we augment, we iterate, we help communicate
9. **NEVER** make unverified claims about speed, accuracy, or pricing without checking claims-and-proof.md
