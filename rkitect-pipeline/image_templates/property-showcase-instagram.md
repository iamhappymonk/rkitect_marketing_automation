**Scene:** {exterior_day_angle} — e.g. front facade, rear pool view, side approach

**Color mood:** {exterior_day_palette} — pulled from the actual cladding and landscape tones of this property
**Camera:** Eye-level, 35mm, portrait 4:5. Building + foreground landscaping. No text in frame.
**Structural rule:** Show at least one distinctive architectural detail — cantilever, window grid, material transition, etc.

## Slide 3 — Facade / Material Closeup

**Scene:** {facade_detail_element} — e.g. cladding grain, window frame, soffit, entrance threshold
**Lighting:** {facade_light} — e.g. raking side light, flat overcast, shadow play
**Color mood:** {facade_palette} — monochromatic or two-tone pulled from the material itself
**Camera:** 50–85mm equivalent. Tight crop, fills frame with texture. One edge or corner for depth. Portrait 4:5. No text in frame.
**Structural rule:** Abstract enough that the material is the subject, not the building shape.

## Slide 4 — Primary Interior

**Scene:** {primary_interior_space} — e.g. living room, kitchen, great room
**Lighting:** {interior_light_quality} — e.g. afternoon side light through floor-to-ceiling glazing, diffused overcast
**Color mood:** {interior_palette} — pulled from the dominant material in this room (timber, concrete, plaster, stone)
**Camera:** 24–35mm, eye-level, portrait 4:5. Glazed wall with landscape view in background. No text in frame.
**Structural rule:** Interior-to-exterior connection must be visible — window, sliding door, or framed view.

## Slide 5 — Interior Detail

**Scene:** {interior_detail_element} — e.g. dining nook, pendant cluster, kitchen island, staircase
**Lighting:** Same quality as Slide 4 — consistent within the property shoot.
**Color mood:** {detail_palette} — tighter crop means this can go more saturated or contrasty than Slide 4
**Camera:** 50mm, slightly elevated or at object level. Shallow depth. Portrait 4:5. No text in frame.
**Structural rule:** One hero object sharp, background soft. Shows craft and material quality.

## Slide 6 — Brand Moment

**Scene:** {cta_visual} — tight architectural abstract, material detail, or clean exterior crop
**Lighting:** Clean, even, no drama.
**Color mood:** {cta_palette} — can break from the property palette for brand contrast

**Critical:** NO text, labels, captions, overlays, or typography of any kind in image.
