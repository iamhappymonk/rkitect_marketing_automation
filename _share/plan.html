Outbound underperforms

Kill: reply rate < 3% after 100 sends & 2 copy iterations.

Mitigation: A/B subject + first-line. Test list quality (intent-aware Apollo filters vs raw scrapes). Switch from cold email to LI DMs.

Influencer flop

Kill: < 50 signups per $1,000 spent on a creator.

Mitigation: only contract micro-influencers with ≥3% engagement in AEC niche. Affiliate share-of-revenue tier instead of flat fee for unproven creators.

Founder bandwidth shock

Stroke-recovery reality: any week may need to pause.

Mitigation: every workflow runnable from intern playbook. Founder owns only "review + close + influencer DM". Bus-factor = 1 but resumable mid-week.

Activation gap

Signups happen, nobody runs a render.

Mitigation: Day-30 onboarding email = "Send us a floor plan, we'll render the first one with you." White-glove first render. Self-serve later.

Pricing wrong

Pilots ghost when invoiced.

Mitigation: pre-pilot pricing chat. Float 3 tiers in a Notion page. Anchor on per-render or per-seat — let pilot pick. Lock by day 45.

SaaS sprawl

Tool stack grows faster than usage.

Mitigation: budget ceiling $2.5k/mo. Add one tool at a time, kill one if adding a second. Track $$$/lead end of each month.
