Before you ask

Four questions architects always ask.

If your question isn't here, the founder will answer it personally on your onboarding call.

Will my client recognise the room?

Yes. Segmentation maps every surface before we render — the sofa stays where you placed it, the windows keep their proportions, the floor plane respects your dimensions. We don't generate "a room." We render your room.

Can I export to Revit, SketchUp, or my BIM tool?

Floor plans export as DXF and SVG. 3D output exports as GLB and IFC. Revit + SketchUp + ArchiCAD round-trip is on the beta roadmap — beta members vote priority.

Who owns the renders?

You do. Outputs are yours, commercially. We don't reuse your renders to train, market, or showcase without your written sign-off — beta members get a one-line opt-in for case studies.

How does it learn my studio's voice?

After every project, the studio absorbs your accepted palettes, vetoed materials, and won precedents. Your sixth brief proposes from your fifth brief's wins — not from a generic library.
