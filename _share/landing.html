How the flow runs

One brief in. The studio runs the project.

01 · Brief

Bring a sketch, floor plan, photo, model, or written brief. The Principal Architect reads scope and intent.

02 · Thesis

03 · Plan

Plan Drafter draws plans + sections. Space Planner sets programme, adjacencies, and clearances.

04 · Render

Renderer produces client-ready options. The Principal Architect keeps trade-offs explicit and routes back to you.

05 · Revise

Each render is routed into controlled edit zones. Click walls, floors, furniture, or lighting and iterate fast.
