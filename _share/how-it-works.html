Discovery flow

Three phases. Zero wasted renders.

Each project type (residential, commercial, hospitality, outdoor) gets tailored questions. The studio gathers site data, establishes a design direction, and validates constraints—all before spending a single render credit.

i

Context

Site diagram drawn on the fly. Type-specific questions surface — orientation, programme, constraints, climate, brief.

Output · Site diagram + your brief, captured in your voice
ii

Precedents

A small set of precedent images, drawn to your brief. Pick one or two — the studio uses them as anchors for the thesis.

Output · 1–2 anchor precedents + a starting palette
iii

Direction

A bubble diagram and parti sketch — the studio's reading of your brief, shown for confirmation or refinement before a single render is spent.

Output · Bubble diagram + parti, ready to refine
