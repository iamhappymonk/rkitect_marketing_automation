# Social Media SOP

## Purpose

Guide social publishing and engagement while preserving brand trust.

## Channel Rules

- Instagram: visual proof first.
- LinkedIn: business value and studio workflow.
- Reddit: answer first, promote rarely.
- X/Twitter: concise, value-first, no first-reply links.
- YouTube: tutorials and workflow demos.

## Review Checklist

- Does this post map to a real workflow pain?
- Does it show or explain a concrete result?
- Are claims verified?
- Is the CTA light and channel-appropriate?
- Does the voice sound studio-native?

## Community Safety

- Do not astroturf.
- Do not fabricate user stories.
- Do not link-drop in sensitive communities.
- Disclose affiliation when relevant.
- Avoid unsupported competitor claims.
