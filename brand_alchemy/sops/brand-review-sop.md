# Brand Review SOP

## Purpose

Review marketing outputs for brand consistency, clarity, credibility, and customer relevance.

## Review Areas

- Positioning alignment.
- Voice and tone.
- Message clarity.
- Claim proof.
- Visual consistency.
- Channel fit.
- CTA clarity.

## Required Review For

- New positioning.
- Taglines.
- Pricing claims.
- Competitor comparisons.
- Guarantees.
- Compliance claims.
- Case studies.
- Paid ads.
- Sales collateral.
