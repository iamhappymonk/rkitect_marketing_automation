# Pre-Publish Checklist

Use this checklist before any customer-facing asset is published, sent, scheduled, or handed to sales.

## Brand

- The asset matches the current positioning.
- The voice sounds direct, practical, and studio-native.
- The message maps to a clear audience and pain point.

## Claims

- Numeric claims are verified.
- Competitor claims are sourced and dated.
- Compliance claims are approved.
- Product capability claims are supported by demo, screenshot, or owner approval.

## Channel Fit

- The CTA fits the channel.
- The format fits the channel.
- Community rules are respected.
- Promotional language is appropriate for the context.

## Quality

- Links work.
- UTM parameters are present when needed.
- Formatting is clean.
- Images have alt text when relevant.
- Mobile preview is checked when relevant.
- Final approver is recorded.
