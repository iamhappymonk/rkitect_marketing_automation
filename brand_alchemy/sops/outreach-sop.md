# Outreach SOP

## Purpose

Create human-approved outreach that is relevant, specific, and respectful.

## Workflow

1. Define the target segment.
2. Qualify the lead or account.
3. Identify a real reason for contact.
4. Draft a short first-touch message.
5. Human-review the first-touch message.
6. Send through the approved channel.
7. Record response state.
8. Follow up only within approved cadence.

## Rules

- Every outreach message needs a real reason for contact.
- Do not fabricate personalization.
- Do not fake familiarity.
- Do not over-automate LinkedIn or other platform activity.
- Include opt-out handling where relevant.
- Do not make unsupported product or pricing claims.
