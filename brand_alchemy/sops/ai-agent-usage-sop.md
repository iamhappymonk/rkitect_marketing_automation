# AI Agent Usage SOP

## Purpose

Use AI agents safely and consistently for marketing work.

## Approved Uses

- Research synthesis.
- Drafting briefs.
- Creating content variants.
- Turning strategy into tasks.
- SOP drafting.
- Template creation.
- Brand review checklists.

## Prohibited Uses

- Fabricating proof, testimonials, or customer results.
- Publishing without review.
- Sending first-touch outreach without human approval.
- Making legal, compliance, or pricing claims without verification.
- Hardcoding brand-specific facts into reusable agents or skills.

## Runtime Context

Agents must load relevant files from:

- `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/`
- `brand_alchemy/research/`
- `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/`
- `brand_alchemy/sops/`
- `brand_alchemy/templates/`
