# Publishing SOP

## Purpose

Ensure every published asset is reviewed, trackable, and easy to maintain.

## Pre-Publish Checklist

- Owner approved.
- Brand voice checked.
- Claims verified.
- Links tested.
- CTA works.
- UTM added when campaign-driven.
- SEO title and meta checked where relevant.
- Images have alt text where relevant.
- Mobile preview checked where relevant.

## Post-Publish QA

- Open the published URL.
- Check formatting.
- Check links.
- Confirm tracking.
- Record published URL.
- Add performance notes after review period.
