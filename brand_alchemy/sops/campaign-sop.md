# Campaign SOP

## Purpose

Plan, launch, measure, and improve marketing campaigns.

## Workflow

1. Create a campaign brief.
2. Define objective, audience, offer, message, and CTA.
3. Choose channels and required assets.
4. Assign owner and reviewers.
5. Build assets together so messaging stays consistent.
6. Run pre-publish checklist.
7. Launch.
8. Review performance within 5 business days of completion.

## Required Fields

- Campaign name.
- Owner.
- Launch date.
- Audience.
- Core insight.
- Offer.
- Channels.
- Assets.
- KPI.
- Risks.
- Retrospective notes.
