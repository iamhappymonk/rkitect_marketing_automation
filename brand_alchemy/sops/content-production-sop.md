# Content Production SOP

## Purpose

Create consistent, useful, brand-aligned content for rkitect.ai and future brand contexts.

## Inputs

- Brand Bible.
- Audience or persona.
- Funnel stage.
- Source notes or research.
- Primary CTA.
- Channel.

## Workflow

1. Create a content brief.
2. Map the piece to one audience and one pain point.
3. Draft using the channel voice rules.
4. Check claims against `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/claims-and-proof.md`.
5. Review for brand voice, clarity, CTA, and source quality.
6. Prepare derivative assets.
7. Publish only after approval.
8. Record performance notes.

## Quality Gate

- One ICP is clear.
- One funnel stage is clear.
- The pain is specific.
- The claim is verified or marked as an assumption.
- The CTA fits the channel.
- The piece sounds like a helpful architecture peer, not generic SaaS copy.
