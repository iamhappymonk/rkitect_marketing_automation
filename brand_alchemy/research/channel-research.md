# Channel Research

## Instagram

Best for visual proof, reels, before/after clips, student portfolio content, and fast transformation demos.

## LinkedIn

Best for reaching principals, founders, design directors, BIM leads, and real estate marketing heads.

## Reddit

High-signal but sensitive. Lead with practical help, disclose affiliation, avoid link dropping, and do not make unsupported claims.

## X/Twitter

Useful for architecture media, AI architecture conversations, competitor monitoring, and peer-level replies.

## WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord

Important for India student and professional communities. Use carefully through workshops, useful resources, and community relationships.

## YouTube

Useful for demos, tutorials, comparison workflows, student content, and product education.
