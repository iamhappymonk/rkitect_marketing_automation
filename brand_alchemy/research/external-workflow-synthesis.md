## Patterns To Adopt

### Single Source Of Truth First

Every marketing workflow should read the Brand Bible and relevant context before producing content, copy, campaigns, outreach, SEO, or sales collateral.

### Stage-Based Marketing OS

Use this repeatable flow:

1. Research.
2. Angle selection.
3. Content creation.
4. Distribution.
5. Analytics.
6. Optimization.

### Stone vs Opinion Intake

Separate confirmed facts from assumptions:

- Stone: verified or owner-approved fact.
- Opinion: belief, preference, hypothesis, or unvalidated input.

### Quality Gates

Before publishing or sending:

- Brand voice.
- Claim substantiation.
- Hallucination risk.
- Compliance.
- Accessibility.
- Channel fit.
- CTA clarity.

### Outreach Workflow, Not Blind Automation

Useful outreach concepts:

- ICP definition.
- Lead qualification.
- CRM state tracking.
- Follow-up cadence.
- Response handling.
- Human review for first-touch messages.

Avoid adopting aggressive LinkedIn automation directly because it can create platform, legal, and account-risk issues.

## Integration Cautions

- Do not copy whole external repos into this project.
- Do not hardcode tool-specific assumptions into the Company OS.
- Do not create global automation hooks that interfere with unrelated tools.
- Keep brand-specific facts in context and brand docs only.
- Keep reusable skills and agents brand-agnostic.
