### Issue 2: No Link Between Research and Claims-and-Proof.md

**Finding:** The research folder makes dozens of claims that are flagged as "Needs Proof" in claims-and-proof.md, but there's no traceability.

**Example:**

- audience-research.md claims "students need portfolio-ready visuals" → No evidence in research.

**Risk:** Claims get used in marketing without evidence trail.

**Recommendation:**

- Every research finding should reference claims-and-proof.md where applicable.
- Every claim in claims-and-proof.md should link to the research that validates (or invalidates) it.

### Issue 3: Missing Customer Validation Data

**Finding:** audience-research.md and competitor-landscape.md read like founder assumptions, not customer research.

**Red Flags:**

**Risk:** Marketing strategy is built on assumptions, not facts.

**Recommendation:**
Run `marketingskills-customer-research` skill to:

### Issue 4: India-First Claim Not Validated

**Finding:** Multiple files reference "India-first market" as a strategy, but no data.

**Red Flags:**

- audience-research.md lists "architecture students" as key segment but doesn't break down India vs Global.

- stone-vs-opinion-intake.md marks "India-first market is better" as Opinion needing validation.

**Risk:** GTM could be misaligned if India market is not actually the strongest wedge.

**Recommendation:**

- [ ] Market sizing: How many active architecture students in India vs US/EU?
