# Audience Research

## High-Value Segments

- Boutique architecture studios.
- Small and mid-size interior design firms.
- Freelance architects and designers.
- Real estate developers and marketing teams.
- Architecture students.
- Archviz and BIM professionals.

## Student Segment

The student segment is strategically important because architecture students need portfolio-ready visuals but often lack expensive software, high-performance hardware, and time. NASA India and college chapters are promising distribution paths.

## Pain Language

- Render times are too long.
- Minor client changes create major rework.
- AI tools look impressive but lack control.
- Clients struggle to understand plans.
- Better visuals can justify better fees.
- Traditional tools are expensive and workflow-heavy.

## Messaging Implication

The strongest message is not "AI makes renders." It is "AI makes render revisions controllable and faster."
