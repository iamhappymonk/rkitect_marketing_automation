# Content Briefing Skill

## Purpose

Create a content brief from brand, audience, and campaign context.

## Steps

1. Load the Brand Bible.
2. Choose one audience and one funnel stage.
3. Pick one pain point and one message.
4. Define the channel and format.
5. Add source notes and claims to verify.
6. Define the CTA and success metric.
7. Use `brand_alchemy/templates/content-brief-template.md`.
