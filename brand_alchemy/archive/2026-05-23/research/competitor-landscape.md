# Competitor Landscape

## Direct AI Competitors

### ArchiVinci AI

- Strength: visible sketch-to-render and before/after content.
- Gap to exploit: post-render control and India-specific workflow positioning.

### mnml.ai

- Strength: architecture AI assistant positioning, style variation, SketchUp integration.
- Gap to exploit: revision-cycle messaging and segmentation-specific use cases.

## Traditional Alternatives

### V-Ray

- Strength: high-fidelity rendering trust.
- Pain: cost, complexity, licensing, render time, crashes.

### Lumion

- Strength: real-time visualization and showcases.
- Pain: hardware requirements, render times, performance instability.

### Enscape

- Strength: walkthroughs and VR.
- Pain: final polish and AI-style revision flexibility.

## Positioning Opportunity

rkitect.ai should position as a complementary AI control layer for fast concept visuals and revisions, not as a full replacement for established high-fidelity rendering tools.
