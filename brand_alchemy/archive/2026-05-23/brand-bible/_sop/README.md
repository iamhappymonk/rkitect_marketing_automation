# \_sop/ — Standard Operating Procedures

This folder contains workflows, checklists, and approval processes for brand work.

## What Goes Here

- Content approval workflows and sign-off procedures
- Brand compliance checklists
- Publishing and deployment guides
- Campaign launch checklists
- Content calendar maintenance procedures
- Quality assurance checklists
- Social media posting procedures
- Email campaign procedures

## How to Use This Folder

Before publishing any marketing content:

1. Find the relevant SOP for your content type (e.g., social-posting-sop.md)
2. Follow the checklist to ensure brand compliance
3. Get required approvals before publishing
4. Document what was published and where

## File Naming Convention

- `{content-type}-sop.md` — e.g., `email-campaign-sop.md`, `social-posting-sop.md`
- `{workflow}-checklist.md` — e.g., `brand-review-checklist.md`
- `{process}-workflow.md` — e.g., `campaign-launch-workflow.md`
