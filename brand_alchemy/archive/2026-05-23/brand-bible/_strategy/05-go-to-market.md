# Go-to-Market & Launch Strategy

_Source: Competitor_analysis_Marketing_strategy.docx, rkitect_master_playbook.docx | Last updated: 2026-05-13_

## 90-Day GTM Playbook

### PHASE 1: Awareness (Days 1–30) — "The Reveal"

**Campaign: "The Hallucination Problem"**

- Create 5 LinkedIn/Instagram posts showing real examples of competitor AI hallucinations vs rkitect.ai output.
- No brand attacks — just show the results.
- Title: "Your AI rendering tool moved your window. Again."

**Architecture Influencer Seeding**

- Send free Pro access to 10 Indian architecture influencers on Instagram.
- Target accounts: Archinect India, Architecture Student Network India, Studio Lotus, etc.
- Goal: Authentic review, not paid promotion.

**Press Release**

- "HappyMonk AI launches rkitect.ai — India's first Spatial Intelligence rendering engine."
- Pitch to: Dezeen, ArchDaily India, Construction Week India, The Architect's Newspaper.

### PHASE 2: Acquisition (Days 31–60) — "The Trial Trap"

**Free Tier Launch**

- Offer 10 free renders on signup. No credit card required.
- Conversion hook: on render #8, show "2 free renders remaining — upgrade for ₹16.50/render."

**Early Adopter Program**

- Invite 100 beta testers to Pro tier at 50% discount for 3 months.
- Gather testimonials, case studies, and usage patterns.

**Partner Seeding**

- Contact 20 top architecture social accounts (Instagram, LinkedIn) with "case study partnership" offers.
- Goal: 5–10 featuring rkitect.ai renders with attribution.

### PHASE 3: Monetization (Days 61–90) — "The Subscription Lock"

**Case Study Campaign**

- Launch 3 full case studies: small studio, freelancer, developer.
- Emphasize time savings and cost ROI.

**Event Presence**

- Architecture conferences, design competitions, startup showcases.
- Demo booth, live rendering on-stage.

**B2B Outreach Begins**

- Target 50 largest Indian builders (Puravankara, Sattva, Sobha, DLF).
- Pitch API for pre-sales marketing and investor presentations.

## Key Positioning Arguments for Investors

| **Market Opportunity** | 1.2M+ registered architects/designers in India. $2.2B PropTech funding globally in 2024. India PropTech growing at 30%+ YoY.                                   |
