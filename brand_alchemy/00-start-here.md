# Start Here

This repo is the internal marketing operating system for rkitect.ai. It centralizes brand strategy, company context, audience research, SOPs, templates, agent rules, and task tracking.

## Source Of Truth

- Brand strategy: `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/brand-bible.md`
- Company operating model: `brand_alchemy/company-os/company-os.md`
- Current company/product facts: `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/company-profile.md`
- Product capabilities and claim status: `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/product-context.md` and `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/claims-and-proof.md`
- Audience and market research: `brand_alchemy/research/`
- Active work: `brand_alchemy/tasks/task-board.md`

## Working Rules

- Keep all durable outputs in Markdown.
- Put raw or source-derived research in `brand_alchemy/research/`.
- Put approved brand truth in `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/`.
- Put reusable process docs in `brand_alchemy/sops/` and `brand_alchemy/templates/`.
- Keep agents and skills brand-agnostic. They should load brand context from this repo at runtime.

## Before Producing Marketing Work

Read:

1. `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/00-START-HERE.md`
2. `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/brand-bible.md`
3. `brand_alchemy/brand-bible/_context/claims-and-proof.md`
4. The relevant SOP or template for the task.
