## Usage Guidelines

- Optimal length: 150-300 characters for engagement
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Best posting time: Tuesday-Thursday, 8am-2pm
- Use line breaks for scannability
- One clear CTA per post
- Add a visual when possible (image, video, or carousel)
