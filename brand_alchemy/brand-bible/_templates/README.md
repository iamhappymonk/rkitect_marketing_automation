# \_templates/ — Reusable Brand Templates

This folder contains pre-approved templates for common marketing tasks.

## What Goes Here

- Email templates (newsletter, outreach, promotional)
- Social media post templates (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
- Landing page templates with brand-compliant structure
- Presentation templates with approved layouts
- Copywriting templates for different content types
- Ad copy templates and swipe files
- Brief templates for designers and external teams

## How to Use This Folder

When creating new content:

1. Find the template that matches your content type
2. Fill in the template with your specific copy and details
3. Ensure you maintain the structure and tone of the template
4. Review against 05-voice-and-tone.md before publishing

## Template Categories

- `email-*.md` — Email templates
- `social-*.md` — Social media templates
- `landing-page-*.md` — Website and landing page structures
- `presentation-*.md` — Slide deck templates
- `copywriting-*.md` — Common copy structures
- `brief-*.md` — Briefs for designers and teams
- `ad-copy-*.md` — Ad templates for different platforms
