# Positioning

## Category

AI architectural rendering and visualization workflow platform.

## Primary Wedge

Post-render AI segmentation: the ability to select and modify rendered elements without restarting the full render process.

## Positioning Statement

rkitect.ai helps architects and design teams create and revise client-ready renders in minutes without expensive rendering licenses, long re-render cycles, or a full visualization team.

## Strategic Frame

rkitect.ai should not be framed as a replacement for architects or as a generic image generator. It should be framed as the control layer for architectural visualization workflows.

## Competitive Frame

- More accessible than high-cost rendering workflows.
- More controllable than generic AI image generation.
- Faster for concept options and revision cycles than traditional re-rendering.
- Complementary to SketchUp, Revit, Lumion, V-Ray, and Enscape.

## Market Wedge

India-first small and mid-size architecture and interior studios, then broader architecture, real estate, and student segments.
