# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Strategic Motion

Education-led community and content, supported by practical demos, student distribution, and targeted outreach to small and mid-size architecture/interior studios.

## Priority Channels

- Instagram for visual proof, before/after reels, process clips, and student content.
- LinkedIn for principals, design heads, real estate marketing heads, and studio productivity narratives.
- Reddit for practical, non-promotional workflow help.
- X/Twitter for architecture and AI workflow conversation.
- YouTube for tutorials, workflow demos, and comparison walkthroughs.
- WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and college groups for India student distribution.

## Campaign Themes

- Stop re-rendering for every client change.
- One shell, many material directions.
- Floor plan to client-ready visual.
- Emergency render safety net.
- Student portfolio render sprint.
- India-ready AI visualization.

## Funnel

| Stage | Job |
| --- | --- |
| Awareness | Show workflow pain and fast visual proof |
| Consideration | Explain control, quality, limitations, and comparisons |
| Conversion | Offer demo, free renders, student plan, or workflow-specific trial |
| Retention | Encourage saved workflows, repeat revisions, style packs, team usage |
| Expansion | Team plans, enterprise/API, developer and real estate workflows |

## Metrics

- Website visits by ICP channel.
- Demo bookings.
- Trial signups.
- First render activation.
- Repeat render usage.
- Revision/edit actions.
- Trial-to-paid conversion.
- Content saves and shares.
- Qualified replies from outreach.
