# Content System & Social Strategy

_Source: rkitect_master_playbook.docx | Last updated: 2026-05-13_

## Five Content Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Education & Insight (35% of content)

Tap into the fact that 86% of AEC professionals believe AI will play a significant role in architecture's future, yet 60% have had zero formal training. Position rkitect.ai as the educator.

**Content ideas:**

- 'What is AI Rendering? A 60-second explainer for architects'
- '5 AI visualization trends dominating 2026 — and how rkitect.ai uses all of them'
- 'Real-time rendering vs traditional workflows: ROI breakdown for Indian firms'
- 'Why 85% of architects say AI saves time in their workflow (Chaos/Architizer survey 2026)'
- 'Post-render segmentation: the feature no other platform has (yet)'
- Carousel: 'Before & After — the same project, traditional render vs rkitect.ai'

### Pillar 2 — Social Proof & Transformation (25% of content)

Clients don't buy software — they buy outcomes. Show results.

**Content ideas:**

- 'Our beta user closed a ₹2.5 Cr interior project using rkitect.ai renders — here's how'
- Short video: architect reacting in real-time to their first AI render
- Builder case study: 'How [Firm Name] cut rendering costs by 70%'
- Instagram Reels: time-lapse of a design going from sketch to photorealistic render in 60 seconds
- 'Sattva-style luxury lobby render: built on rkitect.ai in under 5 minutes'

### Pillar 3 — Inspiration & Trend Content (20% of content)

Feed the scroll-and-save behavior of design professionals. This drives organic reach.

**Content ideas:**

- Weekly 'Design Mood Board' series: showcase a style (Japandi, Neo-Brutalism, Biophilic) rendered with rkitect.ai
- 'The lived-in look: why 2026 renders feel like homes, not showrooms'
- 'Cinematic storytelling in architectural visualization — golden hour, rain, fog'
- Trending design: 'Warm minimalism for Indian luxury apartments'
- Carousel: 'Top 10 renders that would make your client say yes immediately'

### Pillar 4 — Behind-the-Product (10% of content)

Build trust and transparency. Show what's cooking. Humanize HappyMonk AI.

**Content ideas:**

- 'How rkitect.ai's post-render segmentation works — explained simply'
- Founders talking about why they built rkitect.ai
- 'What our beta testers told us (and what we changed)'
- 'We're live on Startup India — here's what that means for you'

### Pillar 5 — CTA & Conversion Posts (10% of content)

Convert attention into sign-ups, demo bookings, and shares.

**Content ideas:**

- 'Launch Day is April 20 — be among the first 100 to try rkitect.ai for FREE'
- 'Tag an architect or interior designer who needs this'
- 'DM us your project sketch — we'll render it live on our stories'
- LinkedIn: 'Calling architects, builders & designers in Bengaluru — rkitect.ai is live'
