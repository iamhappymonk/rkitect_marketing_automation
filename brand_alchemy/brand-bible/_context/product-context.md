# Product Context

## Documented Capabilities

- AI architectural rendering from sketches, photos, floor plans, 3D models, and prompts.
- Post-render AI segmentation for element-level editing.
- Multi-style generation across 50+ styles, including Japandi, Industrial, Luxury Modern, Scandinavian, and Biophilic.
- Floor plan to 3D render with spatial accuracy.
- Auto-furnished room generation.
- Video walkthrough generation from static renders.
- Presentation board export with renders, plans, materials, and notes.
- Workflows that do not require full 3D software.
- Compatibility with SketchUp exports, hand-drawn sketches, and photos.

## Core Use Cases

- Client presentation renders.
- Last-minute material changes.
- Interior option generation.
- Floor plan explanation.
- Student portfolio visuals.
- Real estate marketing visuals.
- Concept approval before final production rendering.
