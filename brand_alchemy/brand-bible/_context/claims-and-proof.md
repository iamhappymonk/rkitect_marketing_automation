## Claim Rules

- Do not use unverified numeric claims publicly.
- Do not imply buildability or technical accuracy unless validated.
- Do not present AI-generated fantasy visuals as real project outcomes.
- Keep competitor comparisons dated and sourced.
- Segmentation claims require product demo evidence before public use.
- Pricing claims require competitor verification (with dates).
- All case studies require client approval before public use.
