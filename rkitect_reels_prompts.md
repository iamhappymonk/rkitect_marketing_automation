# 🎙️ VOICEOVER RECORDING TIPS

- Record in a small room with soft furnishings (no echo)
- Use phone voice memo if no mic — hold phone 6–8 inches from mouth
- Speak **slower than you think you need to** — it always sounds better
- Do 3 takes per clip — pick the most natural one
- Tone guide: warm, direct, conversational — like you're telling a friend, not presenting to a board
